  What is this tool?


 By utilizing Python, Yahoo Finance API and Flask... this tool applies financial market theory and mathematical analysis to give statistics on stock tickers across multiple exchanges, including the NYSE. For example, you could input "APPL", which represents Apple corporation.

What does this data mean?

This is the price to booking ratio. It compares the market cap value to its booking value. If the ratio is under 3.0, then the stock is likely undervalued, and could be one of many indicators that it might be a good buy for you. Companies like AAPL (Apple) or MSFT (Microsoft), likely will not be undervalued, but could still be good investments for the long term, so solely utilizing P/B ratio as an investment strategy might not be a good choice in some cases.
